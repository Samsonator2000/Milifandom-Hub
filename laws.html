Law

Article 1 ~Institutions of Government

The realm has one monarch, which is Archie Beresford. The realm has one parliament, of which all players on the realm except the Monarch are members, unless they are expelled by the monarch. Players may also voluntarily give up their seat in parliament. The realm has one Prime Minister. The Prime Minister must be able to command the largest plurality of members, and is appointed by the monarch. The realm also has one Leader of the Opposition. The Leader of the Opposition must be able to command the second largest plurality in parliament, and is appointed by the monarch.

Article 2 ~Parliament

The Prime Minister may dissolve parliament with the support of a majority in parliament. The Leader of the Opposition may advise the monarch to dissolve parliament, if the Prime Minister can no longer command the largest plurality. Any member may propose a new bill to parliament. A bill becomes law once it has received a majority in parliament. The Monarch may veto any law which is discriminatory or bullying.

Article 3 ~Cabinet

The Prime Minister must form a cabinet once appointed. This cabinet must consist of at least one other person. One member of the cabinet must be named Deputy Prime Minister. Cabinet ministers may be given executive powers over:
- Policing
- Justice
- Public Services
- State Property
- Trade
- Currency
- State Events
- Official State Symbols All powers and responsibilities not allocated to cabinet ministers fall to the Prime Minister. The Deputy Prime Minister automatically becomes Prime Minister if the current Prime Minister is no longer able to. The Prime Minister may delegate any of their powers to the Deputy Prime Minister at their discretion.

Article 4 ~Monarchy

The Monarch must dissolve parliament by the end of each month. The Monarch may dissolve parliament at any point if it acts unlawfully. The Monarch may expel any player from parliament if they act unlawfully. The Monarch must preside over the Supreme Court of the realm as Judge.

Article 5 ~Players

All members of parliament must publicly declare their political affiliation, or forfeit their seat. A player who is not a member of parliament may not take their seat again until the next dissolution of parliament. A new player may join the realm if supported by a majority in parliament, and given royal assent. Parliament may impose special conditions on a new player for up to 2 months, including prohibiting them from sitting in parliament.

Article 6 ~Constitution

The Prime Minister and Monarch may both propose amendments to this constitution. Constitutional amendments must receive a 60% majority in parliament. If proposed by the Prime Minister, an amendment must also receive royal assent.

Article 7~City of New Stevenage

The capital city of the realm is the city of New Stevenage. The city consists of all the land and structures within the original walls and fences, to the west of Lake President. Precise definitions of city borders are decided by cabinet. The city has one Mayor, who is elected by all players on the realm, via ranked choice voting. The Monarch or Prime Minister may not hold the office of Mayor. The Mayor has executive power over:
- Private buildings and new construction in the city
- Official administrative divisions of the city
- Defence and security of the city
- Livestock within the city
- Trade within the city
- Public order within the city
- All transport and travel routes within the city
- Official city symbols

There are no constitutuonal ammendments.

Untitled Law

It is an offence for any player except the monarch to have TNT, or End Crystals in their inventory, outside of The End. Possession of explosives is punishable by removal from parliament for a minimum of 1 month, and community service.

Untitled Law

It is an offence to purposely destroy any property of a player or the state. Destruction of property is punishable by removal from parliament for a minimum of 1 month, cost of damages, or death. Repeated or major destruction of property is terrorism, and is punishable by removal from parliament for a minimum of 3 months, an unlimited fine, a vote for removal from the realm, death and community service.

Untitled Law

In order to be a member of parliament, you must have a fixed address on the realm.
