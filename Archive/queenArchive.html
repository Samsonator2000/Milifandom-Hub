Queens

Since the signing of The Constitution on the 28th of December 2022, the realm has been governed by a monarch, refered to by 'Queen' or 'King' depending on the preferance of the monarch (The latter has never been used)

The Queen is the hhead of state of he realm, being given Operator status and constitutional powers. In return, the Queen is forbidden from political interfearance.
